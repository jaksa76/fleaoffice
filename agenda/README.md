# Agenda

Your meetings and tasks in one unified view.

Agenda brings together your calendar events and task lists, recognizing that meetings and tasks are naturally interconnected. Plan your day, track action items from meetings, and manage your time without jumping between separate apps.

**Key Features:**
- Unified view of meetings and tasks
- Tasks slide into empty time slots in your calendar
- Simple, intuitive interface
- Mobile-first design
- Lightweight and fast
- Data stored via Fleabox

Part of the Fleaoffice Suite - a modern, web-based suite of personal productivity tools.
